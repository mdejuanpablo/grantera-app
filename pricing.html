What's included in the 14-day free trial?

Your free trial gives you full access to all the features of the plan you select. You can find matches, generate documents, and use the Grant Pipeline to see the full power of GranterAI. No credit card is required to start.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will be prorated.

Yes. We take data security very seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We will never share your sensitive fundraising information.
