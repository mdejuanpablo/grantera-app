What is GranterAI and how does it work?

GranterAI is an intelligent grant-seeking platform. Instead of just giving you a database to search, our AI analyzes your nonprofit's mission and programs. It then compares your profile against millions of public tax records (IRS 990-PFs) to find foundations that have a demonstrated history of funding causes just like yours. We then help you write the first draft of your outreach letters.

How is this different from other grant databases?

The key difference is our "Opportunity Score" and AI-powered matching. Traditional databases rely on you to perform manual keyword searches, which often yields irrelevant results. Our AI understands the *context* and *meaning* of your work, leading to higher-quality matches. We focus on showing you funders who are already aligned with your mission, saving you hundreds of hours of research.

Where does your data come from?

Our data is sourced from publicly available IRS Form 990-PF filings, which private foundations are required to submit. This ensures our information is reliable and comprehensive. We process and structure this data to make it easy to understand and act upon.

Will the AI write my entire grant proposal for me?

No. GranterAI is designed to be a powerful assistant, not a replacement for the human touch. The AI generates a personalized, data-driven *first draft* of your Letter of Inquiry (LOI) or other documents. This draft serves as a strong starting point, saving you time and providing inspiration. We believe the best proposals are a synergy of machine intelligence and human passion.

What's included in the 14-day free trial?

Your free trial gives you full access to all the features of the plan you select. You can find matches, generate documents, and use the Grant Pipeline to see the full power of GranterAI. No credit card is required to start your trial.
