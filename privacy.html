Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2025

Welcome to GranterAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy sets out how we collect, use, and protect any information that you give us when you use this website and our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up for GranterAI, we collect information such as your name, email address, password, and organization name.
  • Profile Information: We collect the information you provide in the "Charity Profile" questionnaire, including your organization's mission, project details, funding needs, and other data required for the matchmaking service to function.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how you interact with our service, such as features used, pages viewed, and other actions taken within the application.
  • Payment Information: When you subscribe to a paid plan, we use a third-party payment processor (e.g., Stripe) to handle your payment information. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services, including the AI matchmaking and document generation features.
  • To create and manage your account and communicate with you.
  • To process transactions and send you related information, including purchase confirmations and invoices.
  • To improve our website, services, and offerings based on user feedback and usage patterns.
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests and provide customer service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This does not include:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who need access to your information to carry out work on our behalf (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting providers).
  • AI Sub-processors: To provide the AI-powered features, the text you submit (such as your charity profile) is sent to our AI service provider (e.g., Google's Gemini API) for processing. These providers are bound by their own strict privacy and security policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All communication with our servers is encrypted using SSL technology.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you. Please contact us at privacy@granterai.com to make such a request.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@granterai.com.
